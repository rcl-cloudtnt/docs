# Subscription

Follow these steps to subscribe to RCL CloudTnT

- Click on the ``Subscribe`` button on the main page

- In the ``Subscription`` page, click the ``Check Out`` button

![image](../images/subscription/subscribe.png)

- In the Stripe Billing page, click the ``Start trial`` button

![image](../images/subscription/subscribe2.png)

{: .information }
A Credit Card is not required to create a free trial subscription

- You will need to wait for up 15 mins for your subscription to be processed

![image](../images/subscription/subscribe3.png)

- Click the ``Continue`` link to proceed

# Activating a Trial Subscription

You will need to activate a trial subscription before it expires to continue to use the application.

{: .information }
Your Credit Card payment and Credit Card information is managed solely by [Stripe](https://stripe.com/).

- In the Portal, navigate to the ``Subscription Details`` page

![image](../images/subscription/activate.png)

- Click the ``Activate Your Subscription`` button

- Click on the ``Manage your subscription`` button

- In the Stripe Billing page, click on the ``Add payment method`` button

- Add your credit card number and information

![image](../images/subscription/activate2.png)

- Click the ``Update`` button when you are done

- When the trial ends, the subscription will be automatically activated

- You can check your activated subscription in the Portal after the trial period ends

![image](../images/subscription/activate3.png)

# Cancel a Trial Subscription

- In the Portal, navigate to the ``Subscription Details`` page

![image](../images/subscription/activate.png)

- Click the ``Activate Your Subscription`` button

- Click on the ``Manage your subscription`` button

- In the Stripe Billing page, click on the ``Cancel plan`` button

- After the trial ends, the subscription will be cancelled

![image](../images/subscription/trial-cancel.png)

# Cancel an Activated Subscription

- In the Portal, navigate to the ``Subscription Details`` page

![image](../images/subscription/activate.png)

- Click the ``Activate Your Subscription`` button

- Click on the ``Manage your subscription`` button

- In the Stripe Billing page, click on the ``Cancel plan`` button

- Confirm the cancellation and add a reason

![image](../images/subscription/cancel.png)

- The subscription will be cancelled at the end of the subscription billing cycle 

![image](../images/subscription/cancel2.png)

- Your subscription will be deactivated when in becomes cancelled after the billing cycle ends

{: .warning }
A cancelled subscription cannot be undone. All you data in the application will be deleted. Please download any Open Badges you have before you cancel your subscription.
