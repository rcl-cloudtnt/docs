# What are Open Badges?

Open Badges are digital credentials issued to recipients who demonstrate competence in an area of work. They are normally issued as an image file (.PNG or .SVG) with 'baked in' metadata that provides details about the issuer, recipient and criteria for the badge. Open Badges are standardized in accordance with the [Open Badges Specification](https://www.imsglobal.org/sites/default/files/Badges/OBv2p0Final/index.html).

In this section you will learn about the details of earning and displaying your Open Badges.
